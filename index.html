#noragrets

In 2014, I visited 30 cities in 13 different countries and landed in California with $1.92 in my bank account. During my third year of college, I decided to do a solo Europe trip. Knowing that I wanted to do this, I worked 2-3 jobs on top of being a full-time student and an intern to save up for this trip. Until this day, I wouldn't change a thing! I met many of my best friends abroad, ate great food, had dope conversations about cultures and most importantly, learned so much history!

Some cities I visited:

  • London, England
  • Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • Paris, France
  • Mykonos, Greece
  • Copenhagen, Denmark
  • Oslo, Norway
  • Stockholm, Sweden
  • Dublin, Ireland
  • Venice, Italy
  • Ibiza, Spain
